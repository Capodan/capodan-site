Retail

We analyse how retail language shapes emotion, expectation and brand connection. Whether it’s packaging, pricing or delivery messaging, we explore how customers read between the lines.

Capodan helps teams surface unseen shopper signals, sharpen value communication and build messaging that earns trust and repeat engagement.

Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals

We decode how healthcare language shapes trust, treatment decisions, and brand credibility. These insights explore patient expectations, product perception, and the emotional tone behind today’s leading injectables.

Capodan supports teams in refining messaging, testing response, and building better communication strategies.

Travel & Tourism

We tell you how travel messaging influences trust, spontaneity, and emotional appeal, especially when time is tight, options are changing, or habits are shifting post-pandemic.

These insights uncover the emotional and practical language that shapes bookings, loyalty, and trust. Capodan supports teams in refining messaging, aligning tone with traveller needs, and staying ahead of fast-changing sentiment.

Media & Entertainment

We explore how media language builds relevance, shapes tone and sets expectations in a crowded landscape. Whether it's a headline, trailer or caption, we analyse what holds attention and what gets skipped.

Capodan helps entertainment teams decode fatigue, sharpen distinctiveness and test what really lands with changing audiences.

AI

We explore how AI language builds confidence, signals capability and defines product character. Whether humanising assistants, shaping adoption or framing creativity, the way AI speaks is becoming as important as what it does.

Capodan helps teams sharpen positioning, map user tone expectations and decode what builds trust in fast-evolving tech spaces.

Sport & Fitness

We explore how language in sport builds loyalty, expresses values and shapes how teams and athletes are perceived. Whether shifting tone in fan spaces, reframing rivalry or encouraging inclusion, words carry weight.

Capodan helps clubs and organisations understand how language connects with audiences, supports mental wellbeing and influences matchday behaviour and brand alignment.

FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)

We decode how FMCG language shapes desirability, trust and brand identity in fast-turn environments. From packaging tone to product claims and sensory cues, every word is working harder to signal value and relevance.

Capodan helps teams sharpen positioning, test resonance and stay ahead of category language shifts. Whether exploring AI-generated copy or rising product claims, we give you the insight edge.

Finance & Insurance

We explore how financial and insurance brands use language to build trust, ease decision-making and define value in high-stakes contexts. Whether it’s the tone of buy now services, the messaging of modern investing or how policies are framed, words do the heavy lifting.

Capodan helps teams navigate these shifts with clarity and confidence. We analyse tone, test messaging strategies and uncover how evolving expectations are reshaping what people want to hear.

Public Sector & Government

We explore how government and public sector language shapes trust, signals authority and affects how people engage with essential services. Whether it’s healthcare, digital access or financial aid, the tone of communication carries weight.

Capodan helps teams find the right balance between clarity and reassurance. We test phrasing, surface blind spots and show how word choices shift perception, influence uptake and support better outcomes.

B2B Services

We help B2B brands get clearer on how their language lands with clients, buyers and internal teams. Whether the aim is to sound more consultative, more confident or more relevant, tone and framing matter at every touchpoint.
